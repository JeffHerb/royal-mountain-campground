Campsite Rules

  • Checkin begin at 3:00 PM
  • Checkouts must happen by 10:00 AM the following morning.
  • Ice Cream Store hours of operation are 11:30am to 9:00pm. Store hours are subject to change.
  • Speed Limit on All Camp Roads is 5mph.
  • All Visitors should report to the Office before going to Campsite.
  • Children 13 years old or younger must be back at their campsite by 9:00 PM; Children 7 years or Under Must be supervised at all Times.
  • Adults are responsible for the actions or damage of their children.
  • Quiet Time is from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM.
  • No profanity will be tolerated.
  • Alcohol must be consumed responsibly. No alcoholic beverages are to be the served to minors. Violators will be required to leave without a Refund.
  • No Swimming is allowed in the Pond. No Boats are allowed on the Pond.
  • No Cutting down of Any Trees is Permitted. Firewood can be purchased at the store. All fires are to be put out with water prior to leaving the site or when going to sleep
  • For our Cabins & Rentals only, a Maximum of One well behaved Pet Is permitted. Please remove pet hair from cabins before checking out. If you pet is used to being on the furniture, please bring a sheet or blanket to cover furniture
  • Please have proper vaccination records available for pets. When walking your pet you must clean up after them. Pet must be on a leash and accompanied by an adult at all times.
  • Firearms are to be secured in Campers at All times.
  • Fireworks are not permitted in the campground.
  • No household waste of any kind is permitted to be disposed of at the campground.
  • Management of Royal Mountain Campgrounds reserves the right to remove any Guest or Visitor for any violation of the Rules.
  • Royal Mountain Campgrounds is not responsible for any property lost or stolen.

  • Cancellation Policy: If for any reason you must cancel your reservation, a 72 hour notice must be given otherwise you will be charged for the full reservation. The cancellation fee for less than 72 hours is one night (Two nights for Holiday weekends). Instead of canceling, you may reschedule your visit and the deposit will be applied to the new date.
