About Us

Open May Through October 2018

Royal Mountain Campground is a family run and family friendly campsite

We are located at the base of the beautiful Adirondack Mountains, awaits you with a quiet, friendly atmosphere. We are near swimming, golfing and restaurants. Fulton County is rich in tourist attractions for all ages. Come and enjoy your stay with us.

We have many great sites avaliable including:

  • RV Sites - 45' Max Length
  • Tent Sites
  • Seasonals Welcome

Campground Amenities Include:

  • Ice Cream Store / Light Food
  • Covered and Enclosed Pavilion
  • Sunny and Shady Sites
  • Nature Trail
  • Volleyball, Badminton, Horseshoes
  • Dumping Station
  • Clean Restrooms and Showers
  • Electric & Water Hookups
  • 30amp Electric Service (Two 50amp)
  • Fishing Pond
  • Fire Rings
  • Propane Station
